Cotswold Raspberry Jam

Raspberry Pi computer events for Gloucestershire and beyond

During the pandemic, we are taking donated old laptops from the attics of Gloucestershire, refurbishing them and providing them for local children to keep forever, for remote learning during lockdown and isolation.
